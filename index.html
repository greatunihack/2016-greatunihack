# Developers Designers Makers Hobbyists

GreatUniHack 2016 is a 36 hours hackathon hosted by societies from University of Manchester and Manchester Metropolitan University.
The event is completely free. With unlimited supply of fun, food, drinks and swag .

Learn

Hackathons are all about learning, in GreatUniHack you get 36 hours to work in a team of 4, mentors from the industry and volunteers will be present to provide you with all the help you may need.

Build

Use latest technology to build crazy cool stuff. Use the hardware arsenal we will provide, write a webapp, mobile app, or something completely different, the sky's limit.

Share

Don't let it stop with you, share what you have built and pass along the knowledge and skills you are going to obtain.

FAQ

What is GreatUniHack?

A 36 hours programming event where attendees work in teams of up to 4 to develop software or hardware applications and present their solutions at the end to our panel of judges.

Who can attend?

Due to overnight nature, GreatUniHack is restrictred to students of age +18, proof of ID will be required. If you graduated in the last 12 months, you're still considered a student. Any more than 12 months, you will need to email us as we deal with these on a case-by-case basis.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, a phone and chargers. Other things to consider: change of clothes, adapters, hardware and a sleeping bag, oh and don't forget your enthusiasm!

I cannot code. Should I still come?

You should! Seeing what other hackers are able to make in 36 hours will encourage you to learn even more, we also have wonderful mentors from the industry. If you are still unsure read this.

Who owns my IP/code/idea?

You own all the rights to your IP, we won't claim right over anything created during the event.

I don't have a team, help?

Don't let that discourage you, that is in fact part of the fun, you will get to know 3 new friends over the weekend! We will have team building session let you form teams. We encourage everyone to use Devpost for finding teammates.
